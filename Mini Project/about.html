MCA Department

The department of Master of Computer Application offers a 2 years full-time Post Graduate degree program which is affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE New Delhi with an intake capacity of 60. At Excel Engineering College, semester pattern is followed for Master of Computer Application (MCA). An academic year consists of two semesters.
